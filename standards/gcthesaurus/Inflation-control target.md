# Inflation-control target
> At the heart of Canada’s monetary policy framework is the inflation-control target, which is the 2% midpoint of the 1%–3% control range. First introduced in 1991, the target is set jointly by the Bank of Canada and the federal government and is [reviewed every five years](https://www.bankofcanada.ca/core-functions/monetary-policy/agreement-inflation-control-target/).[^1]
